# Lists (of games)

## The mega-list (2026-06-17)

(Fire emoji means I think it could be actually worthwhile going further with the idea.)

### 🔥 HALF CHESS
At some point I just wrote that in my Things list of game ideas and chuckled, so I could try to make it. In my mind this involved only the bottom half of a chess board and game, so just the white pieces. And that would be it. Just half of chess. If I wanted to be a real prick about it I could make "THE OTHER HALF" with just the black pieces. Hehehe. Or it could be a premium addition.


### 🔥 GHOST CHESS
I already made one version of this (as in the movie) but I was struck by how much I enjoyed the strangeness of the more "boring" version where all the pieces would have a lowered alpha and could just move around anywhere, overlapping as many pieces as you like etc. A bit unclear how easy/hard that would be to represent on the underlying board though? Of course a ton of illegal moves, but also dual occupancy of a single square could make using the chess.js representation of the game almost impossible? Or you would have to have a game instance for every single piece maybe...
 
### 🔥 SLOW CHESS
In keeping with my recent game pack. I suppose you just massively slow down the piece movement and that's about it. Could be kind of chill and meditative? 


### FLIGHT CONTROL CHESS
I've speculated elsewhere in another Chesses list that you could make SLOW CHESS more weird and Flight Control-y by allowing moves *while* other pieces are moving but I think in the end that is just chaos, hard to implement, and... well maybe it's just hard to implement the implications around captures etc. Maybe it's worth it? But I think it would be really hard.

### 🔥 VOICE RECOGNITION CHESS / ENHANCE
Where it does voice recognition for your moves but then just makes some other random move for you. (Would really really need an AI for this though or it really wouldn't work?) I like the idea of this but there are some technical barriers to it. Specifically voice-to-text on mobile (don't know if it's a thing in the browser) and then the fact of needing an AI opponent. I *think* there is a JS chess AI somewhere and perhaps I should look into it.

### EXPLODING CHESS. 
Pieces with a timer after which they will explode with a blast radius. Seems like it might be tough to communicate all the information involved in that though. THis one seems like it falls prey to some UI trouble... a sort of complexity that's inelegant. I think chess probably makes *elegance* a priority actually, which has an impact on the kinds of designs that feel "permissable"...

### 🔥 GRAVES. 
Captured pieces leave a tombstone and that square can no longer be accessed. Might just get too limited too fast. I gave this a fire emoji because I think it's just worth *trying* it to see what happens. THere's a bit of lack of clarity around the capture though. Do you see the tombstone right away? After the capturing piece left? But then why was it able to occupy that square but then no others?

### CHESS OF LIFE / CONWAY. 
Well I've kept wanting to make it so why not want to make it again. THat said I have *no idea* how it would work. It would essentially transform the board per turn which... well there might just be other ways to have that experience of rearrangement like...

### 🔥 TRAVELLATOR CHESS
The idea (and FROGGER is this too) that some squares are moving over time, maybe rows are moving in opposing directions and so each turn the pieces move and you need to think ahead about that. It's very formal, which kind of fits chess. It could travellate you into check?

### 🤔 SCRABBLE. 
Just the idea of like a TRIPLE WORD SCORE you can get if you land a piece on a specific square. Randomized? Like a pawn can become a bishop if you hit the square or something. And those squares could change over time as well? How quickly does it get too complex/messy? There's a bit of a lack of clarity here for now...

### MESS. 
I wrote about this during Chesses 3. The idea that the entire board is just a huge mess and you need to clean it up. But the actual mechanics of that sound tough... what does it mean to tidy up? Felix came up with it again when I was talking to him where his idea was more of a random messy starting position which is far more doable, though I dunno... it would be like a really bad Fischer Random? It's a good title though...

### CONFESS
Well it's a rhyme. Something about telling a secret with each move. Maybe *I* have to have a confession or something since I'm the only one who can do it in good faith? Maybe there's an occasional "confession square" you could access, but it could be a bad move? Would need an AI (that doesn't give a shit about confessions) to make a player bother to care. I suppose you could imagine a universe with a backend database that players can send confessions too and then reveal them on the board.

### GUESS. 
What would you need to guess? Which piece is which? Is there a way it can be like Guess Who, which looks structurally a tiny bit similar? "Can your piece... move diagonally?" and so on. But... that seems unbelievably limited, maybe not enough there for the joke to work. This could relate to the "SECRET KING" idea later on.

### 🤔 LESS 
Just fewer pieces. I small little set around the king and you just see what happens? Or just LESS as in you have LESS pieces that your opponent and it becomes unfair? Both of those are kind of reasonable... both maybe feel like they've probably been done?

### 🔥 LESS AND LESS
Could also do LESS AND LESS where you lose a piece each turn at random. Feels like something to *just make* and see what happens. 

### BATTLESHIP
Some sort of combined version of those two games? Like a battleship moment and a move-your-pieces moment? Or just a setup moment and then battle ship back and forth? There's maybe something too that but fiddly...? I feel like it's a fun reimagining of chess and the sort of "YOU SUNK MY BISHOP!" moment is kind of cool. But unclear how/why you would give a shit if you're not also playing chess. There would have to be a random setup and then what... at one point do you play chess so that something is actually impacted by the battleshipping? And if you play chess don't you then reveal the locations of all your pieces? Or there's like a setup + battleship intro and then you play chess???? It's fiddly right? It feels like I'm making desperate moves to get the idea in rather than it being clear... it is inelegant.

### 🔥 CANDY CRUSH
The one Jonathan and I played where if there are three pieces in a line at any moment they all disappear. My recollection is it was a bit stupid but maybe it was of interest as well? Worth trying. Having re-read the documentation I'm reminded that it needed a special setup because otherwise groups of three match horizontally at the start and decimate your pieces. But that's okay?

### PLAY IT BY TRUST. 
Just implementing the Yoko Ono game in chess.js and so on could be kind of interesting? I suppose theoretically needing to make both sets of moves work at all times could be tricky. And it might be nice to be able to play it? But also weird because, well, it's not my game. To which extent: yeah probably not.

### 🔥 PERSONAL / TROLLEY PROBLEM. 
Mostly thinking as in you label every piece with a "YOUR MOM" kind of label and then just play chess like that. I guess "YOU" would be the respective kings. Quite funny. (I also wonder whether some other ethical framework could be represented on a chessboard for that matter.) I mean, yeah I think this is strong. It's not really the Trolley Problem per se, though that's a catchy name. (Could there be an actual trolley problem?) It's more about PERSONAL or something?

### 🤔 SHOVE. 
No captures by you shove a piece backwards based on the capture rules. Just by one square? Or equal to the amount of "run up" you had. Might be too difficult to do much with the standard starting position. Could you shove a row of pieces rather than just one? I quite like this though I wonder if it doesn't work. Probably have to try (on analog) to find out?

### CHINTZ
I'd hoped CHINTZ had the meaning that CHINTZY has but it doesn't. A sort of "weak chess" would be funny. Like floppy, poorly made pieces, or even the board degrading somehow. But CHINTZ which has the word joke to it, the almost homophone, just refers to the fabric (that came to be thought of as cheap and poor quality) So yeah. Not really. Though doesn't have to be word play if there's something fun/funny about the game falling apart.

### 🤔 RESETS. 
Every N moves all the remaining pieces reset to starting positions? Some other kind of key moment where all the pieces are reconfigured into a (legal?) position? Maybe even a random configuration of the board for every move? That would be trippy. Fischer random but terrible. I kiiiind of like something about it? The weirdness of going back to the start is funny?

### DEATH SQUARES. 
Some kind of bomb lands on a square/column after a set number of turns. Any piece there is "captured" and dead? Or even just on a timer... though then you could screw someone over if their piece was there. Maybe it's a bit dull. Yeah this seems boring.

### 🤔 HORRIBLE STORY GENERATOR. 
Every move is translated into a really shitty story generated via something like Tracery, instead of into chess notation, and you download the story of the game at the end. Once upon a time, a young page strode forth into the world. Kind of thing. Would have to decide what was significant to say for each move etc. Could get overwhelming to make and underwhelming to read. I'd still like to think about it a bit more but it's not obvious how to get it to be of interest.

### 🔥 UNPOPULAR MOVES. 
The idea that pieces have feelings about the move and switch sides gradually, changing colour based on their opinion of your approach to the game. Being threatened scares them, say, so at some point you might have to change your play to make them feel better. Though could be really fiddly to indicate what and why is happening. THat idea of pieces changing sides is kind of fun. Maybe we could just be explicit about what pieces like and don't like? Or it could be a mouse-over thing! Hehehehehehehehe. It's way funnier if every piece has a distinct personality that you can check.

### PROBABLY CHESS. 
All the possible squares/moves are given probabilities and you have to decide which piece to move? I feel like we already had the purer idea of that before with just the random move chess. Yeah this doesn't seem of interest.

### 🤔 FRANKENCHESS. 
The idea you could move a piece onto another piece and the resulting combined piece could do all the things the two could do, but now it's one piece. So a knight-bishop could make all knight and bishop moves. How would you know you could do this? Felix keeps doing this intuitively... I wonder if it has already been made? I do like the tension between a powerful piece versus the issue of it being captured...

### 🔥 ZERO-G CHESS
Mostly it's funny because I made gravity chess but I don't think it's clear what it would mean... just that the pieces all float around aimlessly and you can't play? Is that funny enough? Maybe on mouse over they just get pushed away actually... that's not bad.

### TORCHLIGHT

### 🔥 EDO'S HALF CHESS

### 🤔 HALF CHESS PIECES
